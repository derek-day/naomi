Naomi Martinez is a writer and a member of UNESCO.

Emerging as a promising voice in Caribbean literature, Naomi Martinez hails from the vibrant island of Trinidad and Tobago. Her debut collection of short stories, "Tides of Time," showcases a fresh perspective on the complexities of modern Caribbean life, blending tradition with a contemporary sensibility. Naomi's distinctive style weaves together vivid imagery, authentic dialogue, and a deep understanding of societal nuances. As an advocate for inclusivity, her stories often shed light on lesser-known aspects of Caribbean history and amplify marginalized voices.
